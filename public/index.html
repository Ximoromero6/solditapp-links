What is Soldit?

Soldit helps you buy and sell items nearby with less friction. It’s built for local discovery, clean product pages, and fast actions—so sharing, messaging, and arranging pickup feels straightforward.

Why a new local marketplace?

Local buying and selling should feel safe and modern. Soldit focuses on a fast UI, shareable listings, and a trust-first experience—so you spend less time fighting interfaces and more time closing deals.

Built for links & sharing

Listings are designed to be shared. When someone sends you a Soldit link, it should open the right screen instantly—so the conversation keeps moving.

What to expect

  • Fast posting with a clean, minimal listing flow.
  • Nearby browsing built for local discovery.
  • Shareable links that open seamlessly in the app.
  • Trust-first design with a modern, focused UI.
